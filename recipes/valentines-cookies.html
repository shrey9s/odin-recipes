Valentine's Day Cookies

Description

These cute heart-shaped and easy-to-make cookies are a wonderfully delicious gift to give to your loved ones on Valentine's Day!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 softened butter
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Combine the butter, flour, confectioners' sugar, milk and vanilla.
  3. Roll the dough to 1/4-inch thickness and cut into heart shapes or any desired shape.
  4. Bake in oven for 12 minutes.
