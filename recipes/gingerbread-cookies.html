Gingerbread Cookies

Description

These sweet and spicy gingerbread cookies are the perfect treat for the festive season.

They are sure to delight everyone in the family - kids and adults alike!

Ingredients

  • Margarine
  • Sugar
  • Egg yolk
  • Molasses
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking soda
  • Baking powder
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Chill the dough.
  3. Roll the dough.
  4. Bake the cookies.
