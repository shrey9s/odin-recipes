Fortune Cookies

Description

These tasty fortune cookies are great fun to make for Chinese New Year celebrations.

Get together with friends and family and get creative writing fortunes!

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 large white egg
  • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Write fortunes on strips of paper.
  3. Mix egg white and vanilla extract together.
  4. Drop two spoonfuls of batter 4 inches apart onto baking tray.
  5. Bake in oven for about 5 minutes.
  6. Place a fortune in each cookie.
